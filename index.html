How long does plastic "live" for?

Plastic pollution is any plastic that ends up in the environment – from bottles and bags to less obvious sources like teabags and clothes.

In the past 100 years humans have produced (and used) a lot of plastic.

It's cheap, strong, light and versatile, but all of it eventually ends up in the ground,

in the air , and in the sea – which is where marine animals like albatross, dolphins and turtles ingest it.

Our plastic waste has invaded the highest mountains and deepest oceans.

No one knows exactly how long it will take to disappear, but it's at least hundreds of years.

심플리파이

쓰레기 없는 가게 심플리파이 다소 작은 규모의 제로 웨이스트 숍이지만 주방용품, 욕실용품, 의류, 식기, 식품 등 없는 것 빼고 다 있는 속이 꽉 찬~ 가게다.

천연제작소

마플상회
